Frequently Asked Questions

Which countries can I study abroad from Kerala?

Students from Kerala can study in Canada, UK, Germany, Ireland, Australia, New Zealand, USA, and other popular education destinations based on eligibility.

What are the basic eligibility requirements?

Eligibility depends on academic background, course choice, English proficiency (IELTS/PTE/Duolingo), financial proof, and university-specific requirements.

Do you help with course and university selection?

Yes. We shortlist courses and universities based on your profile, career goals, budget, visa success rate, and post-study opportunities.

Do you provide visa application support?

Yes. We assist with visa documentation, financial proof, SOP review, biometrics guidance, and mock visa interviews for higher approval chances.

Can I study abroad with low IELTS scores?

Yes. Depending on the country and university, options like Duolingo, internal English tests, pathway programs, or conditional offers may be available.

Do you offer post-visa and pre-departure support?

Yes. We provide pre-departure briefing, accommodation guidance, travel planning, and post-arrival support to help you settle smoothly abroad.
